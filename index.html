    Materials Informatics Class: PSP Linkages in Polyethylene using Molecular Dynamics

    This project seeks to establish property-structure linkages in polyethylene by tracking crystal orientaiton and density information through time during simulated uniaxial tensile tests. The data was generated using LAMMPS.
